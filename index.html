How we can help you!

At Paya, we offer a wide range of financial services to help you manage your money and achieve your financial goals. We understand that everyone has different needs when it comes to their finances, which is why we offer a variety of services to suit your needs.

DEPOSIT & SAVINGS

Create deposit and savings accounts in the comfort of their phones. Also, they can link their existing bank accounts for easy management and spending

PAYMENTS

Paya will offer automatic payments to routine services, Installments services i.e. buy now and pay later, and Payments of goods and services direct from Bank

LENDING PRODUCT

Paya will offer personal and business loans with the added convenience of auto payments to alleviate repayment stress. Additionally, financing options for customer purchases will also be available

DEBIT & CREDIT CARDS

Paya will engage in a credit $ debit card product, working with partners like Mastercard and Visa to supply the cards, and merchant/customer transaction relationships.
